How to Cook Perfect Maggi Noodles

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of Maggi noodles
  • 2 cups of water
  • Maggi masala packet (included)
  • Optional: vegetables (peas, carrots, onions)
  • Optional: 1 egg

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pan on medium heat.
  2. Once the water starts boiling, add the Maggi noodle cake.
  3. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  4. Add the Maggi masala packet and mix well.
  5. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until noodles are soft but not mushy.
  6. If adding vegetables, add them when you put in the noodles.
  7. For egg version, crack an egg when 1 minute is left and stir well.
Pro Tips:
  • Don't overcook the noodles - they should be slightly chewy
  • For less soupy Maggi, use 1.5 cups of water instead of 2
  • Add a dash of black pepper for extra spiciness
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves for better presentation
